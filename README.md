# PC Gaming Setup Research

Research and outline for adding a gaming PC alongside a Mac and upgrading from 60 Hz Dell monitors to a better setup.

---

## Context

- **Primary:** Mac
- **Secondary:** Gaming PC
- **Current:** 60 Hz Dell monitors shared between machines
- **Goal:** Better setup — monitors, desk, switching, and PC specs

---

## Monitors

- **Resolution:** 1080p vs 1440p vs 4K — match to GPU and use case
- **Refresh rate:** 60 → 120 / 144 / 165 Hz for gaming
- **Panel types:** IPS (colors, viewing angles), VA (contrast), OLED (blacks, burn-in considerations)
- **VRR:** G-Sync / FreeSync — compatibility with GPU and console
- **Size and viewing distance** — desk vs couch; single vs dual monitor
- **Sharing:** One set of monitors for both Mac + PC vs dedicated per machine; inputs and switching

---

## Desk and switching

- **KVM** — hardware switch for display + USB (keyboard, mouse, etc.); single-cable or multi-cable
- **Software:** Barrier, Synergy — share keyboard/mouse over network (no display switch)
- **Separate peripherals** — two sets of keyboard/mouse; monitor input switch only
- **Cable management** — one desk, two machines; cable length and routing

---

## PC specs (research targets)

- **GPU tiers** for 1080p / 1440p / 4K; target framerates and settings
- **CPU,** RAM, storage (NVMe vs SATA, capacity)
- **Prebuilt vs build-your-own** — cost, warranty, upgrade path
- **Budget tiers** — entry, mid-range, high-end; when to buy (sales, new releases)

---

## Peripherals

- **Keyboard, mouse, headset** — shared or dedicated per machine
- **Compatibility:** Mac vs PC (key layout, software, drivers); Bluetooth vs wired vs dongle

---

## Checklist and references

- **Key specs to compare:** Resolution, Hz, response time, VRR, HDR, inputs (HDMI 2.1, DP)
- **Reviews and guides:** Rtings, Wirecutter, Reddit (r/buildapc, r/Monitors)
- **Where to buy:** Retailers, manufacturer; sales (Black Friday, Prime Day, etc.)
- **Checklist:** Monitor specs, KVM/switch choice, PC tier, peripherals, budget and timing
